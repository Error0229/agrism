User's father (real farmer) tested 快速種植 and found it useless:

1. **Fields are already well-planned.** Farmers don't want a random 2x2m region auto-placed. Their fields have existing regions with specific purposes. Quick Plant should work WITH existing field layout, not ignore it.

2. **Planting involves many considerations.** Watering schedule, placement relative to other crops, companion planting, soil prep — just creating a planted crop record doesn't help. The farmer still has to go to the field editor and manually adjust everything, making the "quick" part meaningless.

**Why:** The feature was designed as a developer shortcut, not a farmer workflow. Real farmers plan before planting — they don't plant first and configure later.

**How to apply:** Either redesign Quick Plant as a guided planting wizard (pick existing region → set care plan → confirm) or remove it and improve the field editor's planting flow instead. Never auto-generate field layout — always let the farmer choose where to plant within their existing plan.
